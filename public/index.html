About Me

My professional life began when I joined the U.S. Navy in 2003 out of Tampa, Florida. My first job was working on F-18's as a computer electrician. After 5 years I volunteered for a special program working the Army filling a detainee corrections officer role at Camp Bucca in Iraq. I then qualified for recruiting duty and chose Chicago as my city. I learned many valuable sales, marketing skills, and speaking skills that would help shape the next 6 years of my life. In 2012 I left the navy and followed my aspirations of entrepreneurship. I started a few businesses starting with an online consignment business where I would liquidate goods from estates and businesses. After relocating to the Seattle area I started a landscaping business that kept me busy for 6 years that was eventually sold so that I could pursue a career in software development which brings me to Code fellows!
